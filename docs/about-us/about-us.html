about aetec

AETEC, Arab Entrepreneurship Training & Educational Co.
AETEC - represented in its training group provides educational and training services in many fields and through experiences gained and developed in various fields. The company is considered a pioneer in these training fields:

  • English language programs.
  • Development Training Programs
  • Computer and Information Technology Training Programs
  • Career development and entrepreneurship programs

Since its inception, AETEC has adopted a policy of alliances and strategic partnerships with major international and local educational bodies to provide the best services according to scientific and international standards.


The company and its training-group are proud of its outstanding academic staff, which has put the company in its pioneering position in educational and training market. Over the past 6 years, the company has trained a large number of public and private sector employees.


AETEC relies on a professional and highly qualified team that oversees the implementation of international standards adopted locally and internationally within effective learning environment based on modern teaching methods and techniques. To be the choice of the largest segment of society.
