Training is a program that helps employees learn specific knowledge or skills to improve performance in their current roles. Development is more expansive and focuses on employee growth and future performance, rather than an immediate job role.

Career Entrepreneurship (CE) is the process of designing, launching and transitioning to the next steps in your career as if it is a business venture. Your career can be about identifying problems and challenges that could benefit from your skills and knowledge and are interesting to you.
